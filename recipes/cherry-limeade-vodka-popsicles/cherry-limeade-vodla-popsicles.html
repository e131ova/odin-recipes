Cherry Limeade Vodka Popsicles - recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups limeade
  • 1 cup cherry vodka
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh cherries

Directions

  1. Stir together limeade, cherry vodka, and cherries.
  2. Pour into ice pop molds and freeze until firm, about 4 hours.
